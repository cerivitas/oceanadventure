Captain's Profile

Jack the Three Fingers

The Captain of the Duchess of Darkness Battleship is Captain Jack the Three Fingers. He was born in Orgdenville, Land of Verhaus. He is a handsome man in his early 20s. His nickname is the Fingers, because he is known to slay his opponent's fingers (ouch!).
Here are some facts regarding Captain Jack:

Height178cm
Weight58kg
Hair ColorBlack
Eye ColorGreen

Skills & Weapons

Captain Jack owns 2 long swords, 3 machetes, poisonous arrows, and several cannons in his battleship. He is a skilled swordsman and ambidexterous, his known techniques are The Claw of Silver Dragon and The Raging Thunderstorm. He is also a good marksman, he is able to shoot accurately from 100m distance.

Law Breaking

As expected of fearless pirates, Captain Jack is wanted in at least three countries, with various bounty amount.

  1. Kingdom of Agaria - Wanted for pillaging, thievery, kidnapping of royal family. Bounty amount: $ 2,000,000
  2. Lanestes Country - Wanted for vandalism and bribery. Bounty amount: $ 500,000
  3. Junteux Commonwealth - Wanted for arson. Bounty amount: $ 750,000

Trivia

  • Favorite song is The Wayward Pirate, which he sang for Princess Agaria before he kidnapped her.
  • Can't cook at all. Last attempt in cooking was when he was serving the public kitchen at the Townhall of Lanestes Country, which ended up in house fire (Which he was wrongly accused of the crime! How outrageous!)
